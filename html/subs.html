BAKING WITH REIYA

Substitutions

There are not many flour substitutions, but in case of an allergy to gluten, gluten free flour is recommened.

Top substitutions for butter in a recipe are: oil or margerine.

Sugar is a very important component in baking and ideally, should remain in the recipe, but any sweetner could replace the sugar. The results, however, may vary.

Baking soda can be replaced with baking powder. It will just result in a puffier cookie.

The milk can be replaced with an egg. If you want to make the cookie vegan, you can use a flax egg or an egg replacement as well.
